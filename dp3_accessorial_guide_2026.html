    <div class="section-header" id="item105">
    <div class="ico">&#128230;</div>
    <h2>Packing, Unpacking, and Crating Services</h2>
    <div class="item-code">400NG Item 105</div>
    </div>
    <div class="section-body">
    <div class="reg-refs">
    <span class="reg-badge reg-400ng">400NG Item 105A/105B/105D/105J</span>
    <span class="reg-badge reg-dtr">DTR Part IV Ch. 402 App. B</span>
    <span class="reg-badge reg-jtr">JTR Par. 051002</span>
    </div>
    <div class="callout callout-gold">
    <h4>&#9989; Pre-Approval Required</h4>
    <p>Pack, Crate, Unpack, and Debris Removal all require <strong>PPSO pre-approval</strong> before service is performed, or must be Government-requested. Verify DPS pre-approval entry before paying any invoice.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="tab-row">
    <button class="tab active" onclick="showTab(this,'tabs-105','t105-overview')">Overview</button>
    <button class="tab" onclick="showTab(this,'tabs-105','t105-formula')">Formulas</button>
    <button class="tab" onclick="showTab(this,'tabs-105','t105-example')">Examples</button>
    <button class="tab" onclick="showTab(this,'tabs-105','t105-validate')">Validation</button>
    <button class="tab" onclick="showTab(this,'tabs-105','t105-crating')">Crating Rules</button>
    </div>
    <div id="tabs-105">
    <div id="t105-overview" class="tab-panel active">
    <table>
    <tr><th>Item Code</th><th>Service</th><th>Basis</th><th>Discount</th></tr>
    <tr><td>105A</td><td>Full Pack / Full Unpack</td><td>Net weight (CWT)</td><td>dLHS applies</td></tr>
    <tr><td>105B</td><td>Pack / Unpack Regular Crate</td><td>Gross cu ft. of crate (min 4 cu ft.)</td><td>dLHS applies</td></tr>
    <tr><td>105D</td><td>Debris Removal (within 30 days of delivery)</td><td>Net weight (CWT)</td><td>dLHS applies</td></tr>
    <tr><td>105J</td><td>Storage Inspection Fee (in lieu of 105A Full Pack)</td><td>Actual net CWT</td><td>No dLHS on 105J; dLHS applies to unpack</td></tr>
    </table>
    <p><strong>Packing/Crating basis:</strong> Rate applied at <em>origin</em> Service Area (block 19 of BL). <strong>Unpacking/Uncrating basis:</strong> Rate applied at <em>destination</em> Service Area (block 18 of BL).</p>
    <p><strong>Full Unpack Rate:</strong> Always calculated at the rate line for Full Pack CWT ≤ 16,000 lbs at the <em>destination</em> Service Schedule × 25% × InvdLHS (58% fixed discount per tariff).</p>
    <div class="callout callout-info">
    <h4>Vehicle Weight Deduction</h4>
    <p>Motor passenger vehicle weight (auto, pickup, van, SUV, dune buggy) must be <strong>deducted from shipment weight</strong> before computing 105A Full Pack charges. Use NADA or manufacturer's weight.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="callout callout-warn">
    <h4>105J vs 105A — Mutually Exclusive</h4>
    <p>Do NOT bill both 105A and 105J on the same shipment. Use 105J only when shipment picks up from a storage warehouse and is already packed. The Full Unpack (billed as Misc item code 226A) is still owed and subject to dLHS.</p>
    </div>
    </div>
    <div id="t105-formula" class="tab-panel">
    <h3>Full Pack Formula</h3>
    <div class="formula">105A Pack = (Pack Rate × CWT) × InvdLHS</div>
    <h3>Full Unpack Formula</h3>
    <div class="formula">105A Unpack = (Unpack Rate × CWT) × InvdLHS
    Note: Unpack Rate = Pack Rate (16,000 lb line at dest. schedule) × 25% × 0.42 (58% fixed discount)</div>
    <h3>Crating (105B) Formula</h3>
    <div class="formula">105B Crate = (Crating Rate × Cu Ft.) × InvdLHS
    Subject to 4 cu ft. minimum; truncate at 2 decimal places (no rounding up)</div>
    <h3>Debris Removal (105D)</h3>
    <div class="formula">105D = (Debris Rate × CWT) × InvdLHS
    Only applies when performed AFTER delivery date and within 30 days</div>
    </div>
    <div id="t105-example" class="tab-panel">
    <div class="example-block">
    <h4>&#128221; Example: Full Pack/Unpack — 6,000 lb shipment, dLHS = 57%</h4>
    <p><strong>Given:</strong> Origin SA Schedule 3, Pack Rate = $54.29/cwt | Dest SA Schedule 2, Unpack Rate line = $5.70045/cwt | InvdLHS = 1.00 − 0.57 = <strong>0.43</strong></p>
    <div class="calc-line">Full Pack = $54.29 × 60 cwt = $3,257.40 × 0.43 = <strong>$1,400.68</strong></div>
    <div class="calc-line">Full Unpack = $5.70045 × 60 cwt = $342.03 × 0.43 = <strong>$147.07</strong></div>
    <div class="result">Total Packing Charge = $1,400.68 + $147.07 = $1,547.75</div>
    </div>
    <div class="example-block">
    <h4>&#128221; Example: Regular Crate (105B) — 4.3 cu ft., same shipment</h4>
    <p><strong>Given:</strong> Crating Rate = $22.75/cu ft. | InvdLHS = 0.43</p>
    <div class="calc-line">105B Crate = $22.75 × 4.3 cu ft. = $97.83 × 0.43 = <strong>$42.06</strong></div>
    <div class="result">Crating Charge = $42.06</div>
    </div>
    <div class="callout callout-info">
    <h4>Internal vs External Crate Billing Limits</h4>
    <p><strong>Internal Crate:</strong> Compensation capped at 3 inches beyond any single dimension of the item. E.g., item 39" × 71" × 0.5" → max crate 42" × 74" × 3.5" = 6.29 cu ft.</p>
    <p><strong>External Crate:</strong> Compensation capped at 5 inches beyond any single dimension. Subject to 4 cu ft. minimum.</p>
    </div>
    </div>
    <div id="t105-validate" class="tab-panel">
    <h3>&#128270; Technician Validation Checklist — Packing/Crating Invoice</h3>
    <ul class="checklist">
    <li>Confirm PPSO pre-approval in DPS for packing, crating, and/or debris removal before paying</li>
    <li>Verify the correct Service Area (SA) for origin (105A Pack, 105B) and destination (105A Unpack) using "Geographical Schedule" tab in 400NG Baseline Rates</li>
    <li>Confirm vehicle weight has been deducted from shipment weight before computing 105A</li>
    <li>Confirm 105A and 105J are NOT billed on the same shipment</li>
    <li>For 105B: verify actual crate dimensions reported in DPS pre-approval; confirm cu ft. truncated at 2 decimal places (not rounded)</li>
    <li>For internal crates (Code 2 shipments): confirm dimensions do not exceed 3-inch allowance per side</li>
    <li>For external crates: confirm dimensions do not exceed 5-inch allowance per side</li>
    <li>Verify correct dLHS discount was applied (InvdLHS = 1 − dLHS)</li>
    <li>Debris removal (105D): confirm service was performed AFTER delivery date and within 30 days</li>
    <li>105J: confirm unpacking was billed separately as Misc Item Code 226A with dLHS applied</li>
    <li>TV crating: confirm TV exceeds 75-inch diagonal screen before approving 105B for TVs</li>
    </ul>
    <div class="callout callout-warn">
    <h4>Common Billing Errors</h4>
    <p>TSPs may bill 105A at the wrong Service Area (e.g., using destination SA for packing instead of origin SA). Always cross-reference the origin BL block 19 ZIP3 to the "Base Point City" tab, then the "Geographical Schedule" tab to verify the applicable SA and rate schedule.</p>
    </div>
    </div>
    <div id="t105-crating" class="tab-panel">
    <h3>When Crating MAY Be Approved</h3>
    <ul>
    <li>Items with unique features (leaded glass, curved glass panels) that cannot be duplicated without great expense</li>
    <li>Marble tabletops and pool table slates too heavy/large for standard cartons</li>
    <li>Flat/curved screen televisions <strong>exceeding 75-inch diagonal screen size</strong></li>
    </ul>
    <h3>When Crating May NOT Be Approved</h3>
    <ul>
    <li>Standard furniture (sofas, chairs, tables, grandfather clocks) unless unique features exist</li>
    <li>Items declared of extraordinary value without appraisal</li>
    <li>Flat/curved screen TVs with 75-inch diagonal or less</li>
    <li>Memory Foam / Tempur-Pedic mattresses (not authorized under any domestic COS)</li>
    <li>Entire shipments on Code D (only individual items eligible)</li>
    </ul>
    <div class="callout callout-info">
    <h4>Code 2 Shipments — Crating Rules (400NG)</h4>
    <p>Additional crating may be approved only for items that cannot fit into an ARTRANS-approved container (approx. 7 ft × 8 ft × 8 ft / 448 cu ft.). All crating must occur at <em>origin residence</em> unless PPSO authorizes warehouse crating. Crates become <strong>customer property</strong> at destination.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="section-header" id="item135">
    <div class="ico">&#127968;</div>
    <h2>Origin &amp; Destination Service Charge</h2>
    <div class="item-code">400NG Item 135</div>
    </div>
    <div class="section-body">
    <div class="reg-refs">
    <span class="reg-badge reg-400ng">400NG Item 135A / 135B</span>
    <span class="reg-badge reg-dtr">DTR Part IV Ch. 402 App. A</span>
    </div>
    <p>The Origin/Destination Service Charge is a mandatory, automatic charge on <em>all</em> domestic HHG shipments. It is an all-inclusive charge covering elevator service, stair carry, excess distance carries, additional transportation, and appliance bracing/stabilization.</p>
    <table>
    <tr><th>Code</th><th>Service</th><th>Basis</th></tr>
    <tr><td>135A</td><td>Origin Service Charge</td><td>Net weight (CWT) at origin SA (block 19 BL)</td></tr>
    <tr><td>135B</td><td>Destination Service Charge</td><td>Net weight (CWT) at destination SA (block 18 BL)</td></tr>
    </table>
    <div class="formula">135A = (135A Rate × CWT) × InvdLHS
    135B = (135B Rate × CWT) × InvdLHS</div>
    <div class="example-block">
    <h4>&#128221; Example: 6,000 lb shipment, dLHS = 57%</h4>
    <p>Origin SA rate (135A) = $3.50/cwt | Destination SA rate (135B) = $6.63/cwt | InvdLHS = 0.43</p>
    <div class="calc-line">135A = ($3.50 × 60) × 0.43 = $210.00 × 0.43 = <strong>$90.30</strong></div>
    <div class="calc-line">135B = ($6.63 × 60) × 0.43 = $397.80 × 0.43 = <strong>$171.05</strong></div>
    <div class="result">Combined Origin/Destination Service Charge = $261.35</div>
    </div>
    <h3>Special Cases</h3>
    <ul>
    <li><strong>Extra stops:</strong> Origin rate = initial origin SA; destination rate = final destination SA; weight = total combined</li>
    <li><strong>Diversion:</strong> Based on BL block 19/18 (not point of diversion). Diverted back to origin = 135A applies twice</li>
    <li><strong>SIT:</strong> Based on BL block 19 (origin SIT) or block 18 (destination SIT). If shipment terminates at storage facility, that facility = final destination for 135B</li>
    </ul>
    <h3>&#128270; Technician Validation Checklist — 135A/135B</h3>
    <ul class="checklist">
    <li>Confirm rate used is from the correct SA corresponding to block 19 (135A) and block 18 (135B) of BL</li>
    <li>Confirm weight used is actual net weight (not gross; not including deducted vehicle weight)</li>
    <li>Confirm correct dLHS was applied</li>
    <li>For diversions: confirm rate is based on original BL blocks — not the diversion point</li>
    <li>For SIT: confirm storage site zip code is NOT used — only BL block 18 or 19 SA applies</li>
    </ul>
    </div>

    <tr><th>Code</th><th>Charge</th><th>Basis</th><th>Discount</th></tr>
    <tr><td>185A</td><td>SIT First Day (includes warehouse handling)</td><td>Actual net weight (min 1,000 lbs)</td><td>dSIT applies</td></tr>

    <div class="formula">185A = (SIT 1st Day Rate × CWT) × InvdSIT
    185B = (SIT Additional Day Rate × CWT × Additional Days) × InvdSIT
    Note: "Additional Days" = Total SIT Days − 1</div>
    <div class="example-block">
    <h4>&#128221; Example: 16 days SIT, 6,000 lbs, dSIT = 55%</h4>
    <p>185A (1st Day) rate = $14.36/cwt | 185B (Additional Day) rate = $0.50/cwt | InvdSIT = 1.00 − 0.55 = <strong>0.45</strong></p>
    <div class="calc-line">185A = ($14.36 × 60 cwt) × 0.45 = $861.60 × 0.45 = <strong>$387.72</strong></div>
    <div class="calc-line">185B = ($0.50 × 60 cwt × 15 additional days) × 0.45 = $450.00 × 0.45 = <strong>$202.50</strong></div>
    <div class="result">Total SIT Charge = $387.72 + $202.50 = $590.22</div>
    </div>
    <h3>Key SIT Rules</h3>
    <ul>
    <li>SIT charges are based on BL block 19 (origin SIT) or block 18 (destination SIT) SA — <strong>NOT the SIT facility ZIP code</strong></li>
    <li>SIT does not commence until <strong>TSP's first available delivery date</strong></li>
    <li>First available delivery date cannot be a weekend or federal/state holiday without PPSO pre-approval</li>
    <li>Maximum aggregate SIT period: <strong>90 days</strong> unless authorized Government extension</li>
    <li>Storage days = day placed in storage + day removed from storage (both count)</li>
    <li>TSP must provide 24-hour notice to customer before placing shipment in SIT</li>
    <li>SIT charges cease on the 5th GBD after requested delivery date if shipment not removed</li>
    </ul>
    <div class="callout callout-warn">
    <h4>Critical: Rate Basis for SIT</h4>
    <p>SIT rates and discounts are based on the <strong>original requested pickup date</strong> at time shipment was offered/accepted — not the date SIT commenced. Always verify the original pickup date from the BL.</p>
    </div>
    <h3>&#128270; Technician Validation Checklist — SIT Charges</h3>
    <ul class="checklist">
    <li>Confirm SA used for 185A/185B is the BL block 19 or 18 ZIP3 — not the SIT facility ZIP3</li>
    <li>Verify SIT in-date = TSP's first available delivery date (not arrival date)</li>

    <li>Confirm total SIT days do not exceed 90 days without Government authorization</li>
    <li>Count days: day in + day out both count; "additional days" = total − 1</li>
    <li>Confirm InvdSIT (not InvdLHS) was used for SIT calculations</li>
    <li>Verify TSP provided 24-hour notice to customer before placing in SIT</li>
    <li>If SIT charges cease due to 5th GBD rule, confirm correct stop date on invoice</li>
    </ul>
